Strawberry Lips

A love poem

Lips that taste like strawberries,
And the warmth of your embrace.
Takes away all our contraries.
My clarity in a world that's full of haze.
Your touch is my weakness,
Yet my strength is having you around.
I'm in the depth without your presence.
What an irreplaceable love can be found?
